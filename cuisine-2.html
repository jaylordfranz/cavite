Tamales

Recipe Overview

Tamales are a traditional dish made from corn dough filled with various ingredients, often wrapped in banana leaves. This version hails from Cavite and is a beloved comfort food.

This dish is perfect for gatherings and celebrations, bringing families together around the table.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Corn Dough 2 cups
Cooked Meat (chicken or pork) 1 cup
Olive Oil 1/4 cup
Chili Powder 1-2 tbsp
Salt to taste
Banana Leaves (for wrapping) as needed

Procedure

  1. In a large bowl, combine corn dough, olive oil, and salt. Mix until smooth.
  2. Prepare banana leaves by softening them over heat.
  3. Place a portion of dough on the leaf, add meat and chili powder, then fold the leaf to seal.
  4. Steam for 30-40 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot, enjoy with salsa or your favorite dipping sauce!

Tips

  • Experiment with different fillings like vegetables or cheese.
  • Ensure the dough is moist enough to hold together.
  • Serve with fresh avocado slices for added flavor.
