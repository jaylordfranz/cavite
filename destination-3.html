Kawit, Cavite
Kawit is the oldest municipality in Cavite. The name Kawit is derived from the Tagalog word “kawit” (hook) which is suggestive of its location at the base of a hook shaped shoreline along Manila Bay extending to the tip of Cavite City.

Aguinaldo Shrine

The historic site where Philippine independence was declared. The grand mansion showcases the life of Emilio Aguinaldo.
