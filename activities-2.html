About This Tour

Experience a full-day adventure exploring the rich history of Corregidor Island and the Bataan War Memorial. This tour offers an in-depth look at significant historical sites, showcasing the bravery of the Filipino and American soldiers during World War II. Your day begins with a scenic ferry ride from Manila, followed by visits to iconic locations such as the Pacific War Memorial, the Malinta Tunnel, and the memorial cross in Bataan.

What to Expect

This is a typical itinerary for this product:

Stop 1: Ferry to Corregidor Island
Start your journey with a scenic ferry ride from Manila to Corregidor Island.

Stop 2: Pacific War Memorial
Visit the memorial dedicated to the heroes of the Pacific War and enjoy the beautiful gardens.
Duration: 1 hour

Stop 3: Malinta Tunnel
Explore the historic Malinta Tunnel, which served as a hospital and command center during the war.
Duration: 2 hours

Enjoy a delicious local meal at one of the island's restaurants.
Duration: 1 hour

Stop 5: Guided Tour of Key Sites
Continue your tour with visits to various historical sites around the island.

Stop 6: Return Ferry to Manila
