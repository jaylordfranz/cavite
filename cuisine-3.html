Kilawin

Recipe Overview

Kilawin is a traditional Filipino dish made from raw fish marinated in vinegar and spices. This refreshing and tangy dish is especially popular in Cavite.

This dish is perfect for gatherings and is best enjoyed with steamed rice or as a starter.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Fresh Fish (tuna or mackerel) 1 lb
Vinegar 1/2 cup
Red Onion, sliced 1
Ginger, julienned 1-inch piece
Chili Peppers, chopped 2-3
Salt to taste
Calamansi Juice to taste

Procedure

  1. Cut the fresh fish into small cubes and place them in a bowl.
  2. Add vinegar, red onion, ginger, chili peppers, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, allowing the fish to "cook" in the vinegar.
  4. Adjust seasoning with calamansi juice to taste.
  5. Serve chilled as an appetizer or main dish.

Cooking Tips

  • Always use fresh fish for the best flavor.
  • Adjust the amount of chili peppers to suit your spice preference.
  • Serve with rice for a complete meal.
